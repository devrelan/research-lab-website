Cross-Cultural Research Hub on Intellectual Disability

Bringing together ethnographic research on intellectual disability across cultures, institutions, and disciplines.

About the Hub

Through more than a decade of ethnographic research in Europe and South Asia, this hub brings together researchers to curate existing work, conduct comparative research, and build global networks.

Global interview-based caregiver study.
